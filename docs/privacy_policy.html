Privacy Policy for Shunya

Last Updated: April 2026

Amanat Singh ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Shunya mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: If you choose to log in to Shunya (via Google or Apple Single Sign-On), we collect basic profile information provided by the authentication provider, such as your email address and name. This is used solely to authenticate you and sync your data securely across devices.

Meditation Analytics: We collect and store your meditation sessions (duration, timestamps, tap counts) and user preferences. This data can be securely synced to our cloud database (Supabase) if you are logged in, ensuring you do not lose your progress.

Device Information: We may collect non-identifying device information to track app performance and diagnose technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Shunya meditation service.
  • Sync your meditation progress and settings across your devices.
  • Present you with your historical analytics and statistics.
  • Improve the application's stability and features.

3. Data Storage & Security

Shunya is built with an "offline-first" architecture. Unless you manually trigger a cloud sync or enable automatic syncing by signing in, all your meditation data remains strictly on your local device. If synced, your data is securely transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption via Supabase.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: For authentication purposes.
  • Supabase: For secure database hosting and syncing capabilities.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. You can perform this action directly within the App's settings, or by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at amanatsinghnain@gmail.com.
